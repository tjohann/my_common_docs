Howto use distcc (see https://github.com/voidlinux/void-packages)
-----------------------------------------------------------------


client side:
------------

xbps-install -Sy distcc
echo "192.168..0/24" >> /etc/distcc/clients.allow
ln -s /etc/sv/distccd /var/service


server side (void-package repo):
--------------------------------

- etc/conf:

XBPS_DISTCC=yes
XBPS_DISTCC_HOSTS="--localslots_cpp=24 192.168.0.80/2 192.168.2.81/2 192.168.0.82/2 192.168.2.83/2 192.168.0.84/2 192.168.2.85/2 192.168.0.86/2 192.168.2.87/2"
XBPS_MAKEJOBS=16
